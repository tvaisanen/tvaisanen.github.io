About

I am a software consultant with seven years of experience from which five years have been working in software consultancies. I have a master's degree in CSE specializing in machine learning and intelligent systems.

I started my career in tech at the age of 26 with my first coding internship year after restarting my academic career. I had studied software development before in vocational school but at the time I didn't really get it and I was more interested in playing guitar and learning music. Therefore I ended up taking a detour in studying music and music technology.

Long story short, because of this I know how it feels to feel behind while other people your age are far ahead industry experience-wise. I can still remember the difficulties of the early years of my career. That's why I want to work helping others make the journey less painful.
